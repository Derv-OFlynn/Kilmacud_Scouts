General History

Our troop was founded in 1963. We have three numbers: 62nd, 71st and 90th.
71st was the first troop and began as an all-boys land scout section of CBSI (Catholic Boy Scouts of Ireland).
62nd and 90th came later, 62nd as Sea Scouts and 90th as Air Scouts. 90th no longer has any sections but 62nd still exists as a land scout troop.
