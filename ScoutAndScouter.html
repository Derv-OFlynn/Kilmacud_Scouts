Explanation:


myScouts.ie is used to register Scouters. It contains information like the national calendar, training days and awarded badges. It used to contain all resources necessary for Scouters however, the website is in the process of being upgraded.


ISSUU is being used to store all the Scouting Ireland resources that used to be on myScouts.ie and is regualrly updated.
